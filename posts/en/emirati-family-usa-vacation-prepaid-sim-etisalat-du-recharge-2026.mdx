## The Problem with Etisalat and du International Roaming

Both Etisalat and du offer international roaming packages for USA travel, but the costs add up quickly:

- **Daily roaming passes**: AED 35-55 per day per line for limited data
- **Pay-as-you-go roaming**: AED 6-10 per MB, which can drain your account in minutes
- **Family costs**: With 4-5 family members, you could spend AED 600-1,000 per week just on roaming
- **Limited data**: Even with roaming packages, you get throttled speeds or small data caps

For a two-week family vacation, roaming charges can easily exceed AED 2,000-3,000 — money better spent on experiences, not mobile bills.
