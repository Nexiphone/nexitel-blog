## Egyptian Banking While Abroad: Why You Need Your Egyptian Number

Most Egyptian tech professionals keep Egyptian bank accounts active—it's where salaries, rental income, or family support flows. Here's the problem: Egyptian banks require SMS verification for:

- Online banking login
- International transfers
- Card activations  
- Account changes

**CIB, NBE, Banque Misr, Banque du Caire, Alex Bank**—they all text Egyptian numbers. If your Vodafone or Orange number dies, you're locked out.

**The solution**:
1. Keep Egyptian SIM card in a backup phone or dual-SIM device
2. Use Nexi Volt for regular small recharges (50-100 EGP monthly)
3. When you visit Egypt, do major banking in person

Some Egyptian professionals port their number to WE (previously Telecom Egypt) for lower recharge requirements, but Vodafone and Orange remain most reliable for international roaming if you travel regionally.
