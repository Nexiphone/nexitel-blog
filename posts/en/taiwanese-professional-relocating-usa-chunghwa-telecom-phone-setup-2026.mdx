## Why Taiwanese Professionals Choose Prepaid Over Postpaid

Many Taiwan immigrants arriving on work visas assume they need a contract plan from AT&T, Verizon, or T-Mobile. Here's why prepaid makes more sense initially:

- **No credit history required** — most postpaid plans need USA credit scores you don't have yet
- **No SSN complications** — some carriers reject applications without established SSN history
- **No deposit requirements** — postpaid can demand $500+ deposits for new immigrants
- **Flexibility during setup phase** — cancel anytime if your employer provides a phone
- **Lower monthly costs** — $10-$40/month vs $60-$90+ for similar postpaid service

Many H1B and L1 professionals use prepaid for their first 6-12 months, then switch to postpaid once they've established credit.

## What About Your 中華電信 (Chunghwa Telecom) Number?

Many Taiwan professionals want to keep their Chunghwa Telecom, Taiwan Mobile, or FarEasTone numbers active for:

- **Banking 2FA codes** — your Taiwan bank apps need SMS verification
- **Government correspondence** — NHI, tax, household registration communications
- **Family contact** — parents and relatives use your Taiwan number on LINE
- **Property management** — if you maintain an apartment or property in Taiwan

### Three Options for Your Taiwan Number:

**Option 1: Suspend Your Service (停話)**
Chunghwa Telecom and Taiwan Mobile allow number suspension (停話) for overseas work. You pay a minimal monthly fee (around NT$100-200) to hold your number without full service. Perfect for 1-2 year assignments.

**Option 2: Downgrade to Minimum Plan**
Switch to the cheapest available plan (around NT$199-299/month) and keep your number fully active. This ensures you receive all SMS codes and calls, though international roaming charges will apply if you answer.

**Option 3: Port to VoIP Service**
Some Taiwan VoIP services let you port your number and forward calls/SMS to your USA number, though this can be complex.

Most professionals choose Option 2 for simplicity — keeping the minimum plan active while using Nexitel in the USA.
