## Why Prepaid Plans Work Better for Nonprofit Organizations

Traditional postpaid plans often require credit checks, multi-year commitments, and expensive family plan minimums that don't align with nonprofit realities. Here's why prepaid makes more sense:

### Budget Control and Transparency

- **No surprise bills**: Pay upfront for exactly what you need
- **Easy expense tracking**: Simplified accounting for grant-funded positions
- **Scalable spending**: Add or reduce services as funding permits
- **No contract penalties**: Adjust or cancel service when volunteer assignments end

### Flexibility for Changing Needs

Nonprofit work is unpredictable. A disaster response volunteer might need service for three months, while a community organizer requires year-round connectivity. Prepaid plans adapt instantly—no calls to customer service, no early termination fees.
