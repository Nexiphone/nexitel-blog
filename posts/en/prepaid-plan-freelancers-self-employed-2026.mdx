## Data Management Tips for Freelancers

Maximize your plan's value with these data-saving strategies:

### When Working on Mobile Data:
- Download important files while on Wi-Fi before leaving
- Use data compression in your mobile browser
- Disable auto-play videos on social media
- Leverage offline modes in productivity apps

### Optimize Your Hotspot Usage:
- Connect only essential devices
- Avoid streaming video content
- Schedule large uploads for Wi-Fi environments
- Monitor data usage in your phone settings

These habits help you stay within your plan's allocation or consider upgrading only when truly necessary.
