## Optimizing Your Mobile Setup for Field Work

### Data Management Tips

Even with generous data allowances, smart usage extends your capabilities:

1. **Download policy documents on WiFi** before heading to appointments
2. **Use offline maps** to reduce data consumption during navigation
3. **Compress photos** before uploading to claim management systems
4. **Schedule video calls** for WiFi locations when possible
5. **Monitor your usage** through Nexitel's account portal

### Professional Communication Best Practices

Your mobile phone is your primary business tool. Maintain professionalism:

- Set up professional voicemail greetings
- Use separate lines for business and personal calls when possible
- Enable call recording (where legal) for accurate claim documentation
- Maintain backup power with portable chargers
- Test coverage in your key territories before committing
