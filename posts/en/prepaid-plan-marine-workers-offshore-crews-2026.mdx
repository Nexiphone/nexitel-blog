## Why Marine Workers Need Specialized Prepaid Plans

Life at sea means unpredictable schedules, extended deployments, and limited opportunities to manage phone service. Whether you're working on oil rigs, commercial fishing vessels, cargo ships, or cruise lines, you need a mobile plan that works when you're on shore leave—without paying for service while you're offshore.

Traditional postpaid plans don't make sense for maritime professionals. You're paying monthly fees even when your phone sits in a locker for weeks or months at a time. Prepaid plans offer the flexibility marine workers need: activate service when you dock, pause when you ship out, and never worry about contracts or credit checks.

## What Marine Workers Should Look for in a Prepaid Plan

### Flexibility and No Contracts

The most critical feature for offshore crews is the ability to control when you're paying for service. With prepaid plans, you only pay for the months you need coverage. No cancellation fees, no explaining your work schedule to customer service, no wasted money.

### Nationwide Coverage for Multiple Ports

Your ship might dock in Houston this month, Seattle next month, and Miami the month after. You need reliable coverage across all U.S. ports and coastal cities. Network quality matters when you're making important calls to family, handling banking, or coordinating travel.

### Affordable Data and Talk Plans

Shore leave is expensive enough without overpaying for phone service. The best plans for marine workers offer substantial data, unlimited talk and text, and pricing that respects your budget—especially since you're not using service year-round.

### International Calling Options

Many maritime professionals have family overseas or work with international crews. Having affordable international calling can be essential for staying connected with loved ones during your limited time ashore.

## How to Use Prepaid Plans Between Deployments

### Activation Strategy for Offshore Workers

Here's how marine workers maximize value with Nexitel prepaid plans:

1. **Before Shore Leave**: Activate your plan online before docking or immediately upon arrival
2. **During Shore Leave**: Use your service normally—calls, texts, data, apps
3. **Before Shipping Out**: Simply let your plan expire; no cancellation needed
4. **Next Shore Leave**: Reactivate with the same number or get a new one

This flexibility means you're never paying for service you can't use. Your SIM card remains valid, and many workers keep the same number by reactivating before the previous service expires.

### Multi-Month Planning for Longer Rotations

If you're on a predictable rotation (like 28 days on, 28 days off), you can plan your prepaid service accordingly. Stock up on refill cards or set reminders to activate before each shore rotation. Since there are no contracts, you control exactly when service is active.

## Tips for Marine Workers Using Prepaid Service

### Keep Your SIM Card Safe

Store your SIM card in a secure location aboard ship. Many workers keep it with important documents or in their phone with the device powered off. This way, you're ready to activate as soon as you reach port.

### Consider a Backup Phone

Having an inexpensive backup smartphone dedicated to your Nexitel SIM means you don't need to worry about your primary device during deployment. Activate the backup phone during shore leave and keep your main phone for personal use.

### Use Wi-Fi Calling When Possible

Both AT&T and T-Mobile networks support Wi-Fi calling on compatible devices. When you're in port but aboard ship with Wi-Fi access, you can save data by using Wi-Fi calling features.

### Plan for Data Needs

If you need to download updates, stream content, or handle large data tasks during shore leave, consider the higher-tier plans. The unlimited data options ($25-30/month) provide peace of mind for data-intensive needs during your limited time ashore.
