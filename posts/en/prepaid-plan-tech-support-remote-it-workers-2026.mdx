### Remote Desktop and VPN Connectivity

Modern remote desktop protocols like RDP, VNC, and cloud-based solutions require surprisingly modest bandwidth when properly configured. A stable 3-5 Mbps connection handles most remote administration tasks smoothly. However, data consumption varies:

- Basic remote desktop session: 50-150 MB per hour
