## Practical Tips for Apartment Renters Choosing Prepaid Plans

### Test Coverage in Your Building First

Before committing, check coverage in your specific apartment:

1. Ask neighbors which carriers work best in your building
2. Test signal strength in your unit, basement laundry room, and parking garage
3. Verify Wi-Fi calling works properly if your building has signal challenges
4. Consider that higher floors may have better reception

### Calculate Your Actual Data Needs

Most apartment renters overestimate their mobile data usage:

- Connect to apartment Wi-Fi for streaming, downloads, and updates
- Use Wi-Fi for video calls and large file transfers
- Reserve mobile data for when you're out and about
- Track usage for one month to determine your real needs

Many renters find that 3GB or 6GB plans provide plenty of data when combined with home Wi-Fi usage.
