### Primary Applicant (H1B/L1/Green Card Holder)

You need reliable service for work calls, interviews, banking OTPs, and daily communication. Whether you're in IT consulting traveling between client sites or working from a fixed office location, consistent coverage matters.

Your spouse needs connectivity for job searches (if H4 EAD approved), kids' school communication, doctor appointments, and staying in touch with family in India. Each family member doesn't need an expensive $50-60/month plan.

When your parents visit for 3-6 months, they need basic calling and WhatsApp connectivity without paying premium prices for service they'll use temporarily.
