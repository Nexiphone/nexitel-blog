## Understanding Your Communication Needs as a Wellness Professional

Before selecting a prepaid plan, assess your typical phone usage:

- **Client calls**: How many minutes do you spend on coaching calls per month?
- **Text messaging**: Do you use SMS for appointment reminders and motivational messages?
- **Data usage**: Are you sharing wellness resources, hosting virtual sessions, or using practice management apps?
- **Scheduling flexibility**: Do your busiest seasons require more connectivity than others?

Most life coaches and wellness consultants find they need moderate data (5-15GB), unlimited talk and text, and the flexibility to adjust plans as their practice grows.

## Maximizing Your Prepaid Plan for Business Success

3. **Implement text reminders** for appointments to reduce no-shows

- Use Wi-Fi for longer video sessions when possible
- Download client resources while on Wi-Fi to share later
- Monitor your data usage through your phone's settings
- Upgrade temporarily during busy months if needed
