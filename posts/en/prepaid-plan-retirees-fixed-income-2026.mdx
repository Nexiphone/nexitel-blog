## What Matters Most for Retirees

Different lifestyles demand different plan features. Here's what to focus on:

- **Coverage** — Does the network reach where you spend most of your time?
- **Data needs** — Light browsing or heavy streaming and uploads?
- **Talk and text** — Unlimited domestic, plus options for international calls
- **Hotspot** — Useful when you need to tether a laptop or tablet
- **Price** — Predictable monthly cost with no surprise fees
