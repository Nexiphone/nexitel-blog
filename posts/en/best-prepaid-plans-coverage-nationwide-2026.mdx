## How Prepaid Coverage Actually Works

A common misconception is that prepaid customers get worse coverage than postpaid subscribers. Here is the reality:

- **Same towers** — Nexitel plans use the same cell towers as AT&T and T-Mobile postpaid customers
- **Same frequencies** — you get access to the same LTE and 5G bands
- **Same geographic footprint** — if AT&T or T-Mobile covers your area, so does Nexitel
- **Data prioritization** — during extreme network congestion, postpaid users may get temporary priority, but in normal conditions there is no difference

This means you can get top-tier nationwide coverage while paying prepaid prices.
