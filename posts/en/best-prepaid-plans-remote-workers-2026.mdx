## Using Your Phone as a Backup Internet Source

Every remote worker's nightmare is a home internet outage during an important meeting or deadline. A prepaid phone plan with hotspot capability is your insurance policy.

### How to set up your backup:

1. **Enable mobile hotspot** in your phone's settings
2. **Connect your laptop** to the phone's Wi-Fi network
3. **Continue working** as if nothing happened

With a data-sufficient Nexitel plan, you can run video calls, access cloud storage, and send emails through your phone's hotspot. The key is choosing a plan tier with enough data to handle this usage without throttling.

### Data usage estimates for remote work:

- **Video call (Zoom/Meet/Teams)** — about 1-2 GB per hour
- **Email and messaging** — under 100 MB per day
- **Cloud file access (Google Drive, Dropbox)** — varies, but typically 200-500 MB per day
- **General web browsing** — about 200 MB per day
- **VoIP calls** — roughly 40 MB per hour

For a full workday on hotspot, plan for 5-10 GB. If you only need it for emergencies, a lighter data tier works fine.
