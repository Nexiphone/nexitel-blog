## What You Should Expect From a $15 Plan

Before picking any cheap plan, make sure it ticks these boxes:

1. **Unlimited talk and text** — not just a limited bucket of minutes
2. **Real 4G LTE or 5G data** — no artificial throttling below reasonable speeds
3. **A usable data bucket** — enough for maps, messaging, and occasional video
4. **No contracts** — month-to-month only, cancel anytime
5. **Nationwide coverage** — on a major network, not a hidden regional one
6. **Number portability** — keep your existing phone number

If any of those are missing, the $15 price tag is probably hiding a catch. Nexitel's entry tiers include all of the above.
