## Transportation Safety for Thursday Evenings

Getting home safely is often the most vulnerable part of a Thursday night out. Poor transportation decisions can undo all your earlier safety precautions.

### Rideshare Best Practices

When using rideshare services, always verify the driver's identity, license plate, and car model before entering. Sit in the back seat, and share your trip details with a friend. Keep your phone in your hand throughout the ride, and trust your instincts—if something feels wrong, end the ride in a safe, public location.

Before getting into any rideshare on Thursday night, activate One Tap Alert's Safety Timer with an estimated arrival time. This ensures your emergency contacts will be notified if you don't make it home when expected. ### Public Transportation Considerations

If using public transit on Thursday evenings, stay in well-populated cars or areas of stations. Keep your phone accessible and remain aware of your surroundings. Avoid displaying expensive items, and have your route planned before boarding.

### Walking Safety

If walking any distance, stick to well-lit, populated routes. Walk with confidence and purpose, keeping your head up and remaining aware. Keep your phone accessible but avoid being absorbed in it to the point where you're not paying attention to your environment.

## Thursday-Specific Venue Safety Tips

Different types of Thursday venues present unique safety considerations.

### Bar and Club Safety

Choose reputable establishments with visible security presence. Stay with your group when moving through the venue, and establish regular check-in times. Be cautious in bathrooms, coat check areas, and smoking sections where you're more isolated from your friends.

### Restaurant Happy Hours

While restaurant environments often feel safer than clubs, they present their own risks. Parking lots can be isolated, especially if you've stayed into the evening hours. Be aware of who notices when you pay your bill, as this can make you a target in the parking area.

Before walking to your car after a Thursday happy hour, activate One Tap Alert's Safety Timer. This ensures that if something happens in the parking lot or during your drive home, your contacts will be notified within your specified timeframe.

### Outdoor Events and Rooftops

Thursday outdoor events offer beautiful atmospheres but also present challenges like multiple entry/exit points, darker areas, and crowds. Keep your group together, and be especially cautious near edges, stairs, and isolated spots.
