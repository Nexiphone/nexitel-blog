## Maximizing Your Data Allowance

Smart data management helps pet care professionals stay within plan limits:

1. **Download maps for offline use**: Reduce GPS data consumption by pre-downloading service area maps
2. **Use Wi-Fi for large uploads**: Send bulk photos and videos from home using Wi-Fi before heading out
3. **Compress images**: Use apps that automatically compress photos before sending to clients
4. **Schedule updates**: Batch your client communications instead of constant streaming
5. **Monitor usage**: Check your data consumption weekly to avoid surprises

Most dog walkers find that with these simple habits, even a 5GB plan provides plenty of headroom for professional pet care communications.
