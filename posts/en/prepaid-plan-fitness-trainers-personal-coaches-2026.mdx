## Tips for Managing Data as a Fitness Trainer

### Optimize Your Mobile Usage

1. **Download music playlists** when on WiFi for offline streaming during sessions
2. **Use WiFi at gyms** for uploading large video files and content
3. **Schedule social media posts** in batches when connected to WiFi
4. **Download workout videos** for offline access rather than streaming
5. **Use data monitoring apps** to track usage patterns and avoid surprises
6. **Connect to client WiFi** during home visits when appropriate
7. **Pre-load maps and routes** for outdoor training locations
