## Tips for Maximizing Your Streaming Plan

### Data Management Strategies

1. **Stream at appropriate bitrates** — Don't over-compress or waste data on excessive quality
2. **Use WiFi when available** — Save mobile data for truly mobile situations
3. **Download content during off-hours** — Prepare clips and overlays on WiFi
4. **Monitor usage patterns** — Adjust your plan tier based on actual consumption
5. **Cache social media content** — Reduce redundant data usage

- Use network monitoring tools to identify issues
- Configure QoS settings on your hotspot device
- Keep streaming software updated for efficiency improvements
