## Data-Saving Tips for Market Days

Stretch your data further with these vendor-tested strategies:

1. **Download content before you leave home**: Preload music playlists, maps, and any digital inventory you need
2. **Use venue WiFi when available**: Many indoor markets and community centers offer free WiFi
3. **Schedule posts in advance**: Use apps like Later or Buffer to schedule social media posts when you have WiFi
4. **Optimize photo sizes**: Compress images before uploading to social media
5. **Disable auto-play videos**: Prevent apps from automatically playing videos in your feed
6. **Use lite versions of apps**: Facebook Lite, Instagram Lite, and other "lite" apps use significantly less data
