## Before You Begin

Activating a new phone plan used to mean a trip to a carrier store and an hour of paperwork. In 2026, activating a [Nexitel plan](https://nexitel.us/plans) takes less than ten minutes from your couch. Before you start, have these things ready:

- An unlocked phone that supports 5G (most phones from 2019 onward)
- Your Nexitel account email and confirmation
- A reliable Wi-Fi connection for the setup
- Your current phone number if you are porting in

## Step 1 – Choose Your Plan and Network

Decide whether you want AT&T 5G or T-Mobile 5G coverage:

- [Nexitel Purple plans](https://nexitel.us/purple-plans) run on T-Mobile 5G, starting at $6/mo
- [Nexitel Blue plans](https://nexitel.us/blue-plans) run on AT&T 5G, starting at $10/mo

Pick based on which network has the strongest coverage at your home, workplace, and common routes.

## Step 2 – eSIM or Physical SIM

### eSIM (Recommended)

If your phone supports eSIM (iPhone XS and newer, Pixel 3 and newer, most recent Samsung Galaxy), choose eSIM at checkout. You will receive a QR code or an activation link. No shipping wait.

### Physical SIM

If your phone does not support eSIM, or you prefer a physical card, select the SIM shipping option. Most orders arrive in 2-5 business days.

## Step 3 – Activate Your SIM

### For eSIM

1. Open Settings on your phone
2. Go to Cellular/Mobile Data → Add Plan
3. Scan the QR code from your Nexitel welcome email
4. Follow the prompts to label the line
5. Wait for the signal bars to appear

### For Physical SIM

1. Power off your phone
2. Insert the new SIM
3. Power the phone back on
4. Follow the on-screen carrier setup prompts
5. Reboot if needed

## Step 4 – Port in Your Old Number (Optional)

If you want to keep your existing number, you will need:

- The old carrier's account number
- The transfer PIN from the old carrier (usually a text command or support call)
- Your old phone number and billing ZIP

Enter these during activation. Ports usually complete within 1 hour, sometimes up to 24.

## Step 5 – Test Your Connection

- Make a test call to a friend
- Send an SMS
- Load a 5G speed test website
- Check that iMessage or RCS is enabled

## Step 6 – Set Up NexiTalk (Optional)

If you call internationally, add [NexiTalk VoIP](https://nexitel.us/plans) at $4.99/mo and sign in to the NexiTalk app. It works on Wi-Fi and on your new Nexitel data.

## Common Issues and Fixes

### No Signal After Activation

Toggle airplane mode on and off, then reboot. If still no signal, confirm the line shows as "Active" in your Nexitel account.

### SIM Not Detected

Clean the SIM tray and reinsert. If it is still not detected, request a replacement from support.

### Hotspot Not Working

Verify your plan tier includes hotspot, then reset network settings.

Activating a new Nexitel plan is meant to take minutes, not hours. With [Nexitel plans](https://nexitel.us/plans) from $6/mo, you can switch from a postpaid contract to a flexible prepaid line over your morning coffee.
