## Data Management Tips for Podcasters

Even with unlimited data, smart data management extends your phone's battery life and ensures optimal upload performance:

1. **Upload during off-peak hours** when network congestion is lower (early morning or late evening)
2. **Use WiFi for initial backups** before relying on mobile data for final uploads
3. **Compress files appropriately** without sacrificing audio quality—128kbps is sufficient for most speech-based podcasts
4. **Queue uploads** rather than attempting simultaneous transfers to multiple platforms
5. **Monitor upload progress** to catch failures early and retry while still in good coverage
