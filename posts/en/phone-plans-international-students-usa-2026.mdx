## Getting a US Phone Number as an International Student

The minute you land at JFK, LAX, or ORD, your top priorities are simple: a SIM card, a ride to campus, and Wi-Fi. A local US phone number makes all of that easier. It is required for:

- Opening a US bank account
- Setting up two-factor authentication for your student portal
- Signing a lease or dorm agreement
- Ride-share apps, food delivery, and campus services
- Staying in touch with classmates

Traditional postpaid carriers often require a US Social Security Number and a credit history. As a new international student, you usually do not have either. That is why prepaid is the smart first move.
