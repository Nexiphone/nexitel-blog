## Phone Compatibility: Will Your Parents' Phone Work?

Most modern Indian smartphones work in USA, but verify:

**Guaranteed to Work:**
- iPhones (iPhone 6S and newer)
- Samsung Galaxy (S8 and newer)
- OnePlus (5T and newer)
- Google Pixel (all models)

**Check Before Travel:**
- Phone must be **unlocked** (not carrier-locked)
- Should support **4G LTE bands 2, 4, 5, 12, 66, 71** (most 2020+ phones do)
- **CDMA phones** (very old Reliance phones) won't work

**Need to unlock their phone?** In India, call Jio/Airtel customer care and request unlocking 2-3 weeks before travel. It's usually free after contract period.
