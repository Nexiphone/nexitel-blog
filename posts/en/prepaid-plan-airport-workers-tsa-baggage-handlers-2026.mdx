### Terminal Buildings and Underground Areas

Modern airports feature thick concrete walls, steel reinforcements, and underground corridors that can block cellular signals. TSA agents working in basement-level screening areas or baggage handlers in subterranean sorting facilities need carriers with robust infrastructure.

**Nexitel Blue Plans** operate on the AT&T network, offering extensive coverage across 99% of Americans. AT&T's network infrastructure includes enhanced building penetration technology, making it ideal for airport terminals.

### Tarmac and Airfield Operations

Ground crew members, refueling technicians, and aircraft maintenance workers spend significant time on open tarmacs. These areas require different coverage characteristics than indoor spaces. Both AT&T and T-Mobile networks provide excellent outdoor coverage at major airports nationwide.
