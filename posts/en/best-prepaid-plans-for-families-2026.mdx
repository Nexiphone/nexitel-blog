## What Makes a Good Family Prepaid Plan?

Not every prepaid provider is built for multi-line households. When evaluating options, look for these essentials:

- **Per-line pricing under $25** — each additional line should cost less, not more
- **No contracts** — month-to-month flexibility so you can adjust as kids grow or needs change
- **Reliable network coverage** — plans backed by AT&T or T-Mobile towers, not a small regional carrier
- **Mix-and-match tiers** — not every family member needs the same amount of data
- **Number portability** — keep everyone's existing phone numbers
- **Simple account management** — one dashboard to manage all lines

## Tips for Managing a Family Prepaid Account

1. **Set data alerts** — help kids learn to manage their own usage
2. **Use Wi-Fi calling** — reduce data consumption at home
3. **Review usage monthly** — switch tiers up or down as needed with no penalty
4. **Port numbers early** — start the number transfer process before your current billing cycle ends
