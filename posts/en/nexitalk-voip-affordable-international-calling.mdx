## What Is VoIP and Why Does It Matter?

VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) routes your calls through the internet rather than traditional phone networks. The practical result: dramatically lower calling costs, especially for international destinations. You keep your existing number, you still use your smartphone, and the call quality on a solid Wi-Fi or LTE connection is comparable to any standard call.

NexiTalk works on any Android or iOS device and doesn't require a new SIM or carrier switch. It's an add-on service you can pair with any existing Nexitel plan — or use as a standalone calling solution.

**Immigrants and expats** are the clearest use case. Staying connected with family back home is non-negotiable — but it shouldn't cost $50 or more per month just in calling fees. NexiTalk keeps those conversations affordable so you don't have to ration them.

NexiTalk at $4.99–$19.99/month, depending on your usage level, represents a meaningful savings — particularly for households with multiple people calling the same country.
